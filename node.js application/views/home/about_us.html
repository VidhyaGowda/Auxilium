About Us

Project Auxilium was developed in the midst of the Covid-19 pandemic to help service providers get in touch with the people who needed these services. An Individual can host services like Offering Food, Jobs or Shelter to help the people around them who lost their jobs and homes because of the pademic or due to a natural calmity. Our mission statement is 'To Help you Help others'. Our tag line is 'Leave a footprint of Kindness' because we believe that the ones who get helped today will help someone else another day and together, make the world a better place.

Provide services

If you are interested in helping someone around you, click on the 'Post a new service' under the 'Provide services' tab, choose a category you wish to provide the service under, add a description about the service you want to provide and click on 'Post a new service'. You will be prompted a map to choose the location where you will be hosting your service and click on 'Select the Location'. Now your service is posted and it can be viewed in the 'Recent services posted' button under the 'Provide services' tab.

Need services

If you are looking for services around you like Food distribution, Small Jobs or a temporary place to stay, you can click on the 'Find services' button under the 'Need services?' tab, type the service you want and click on the 'Search' button. A map will pop up for you to input your current location. You will get a list of related services near you. Choose the service you want and head to the location pointed on the map.
